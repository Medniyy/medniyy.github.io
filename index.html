How we work
  1. 01 · Day 0
    15-minute intro call
    You tell us the event, the goal, the timeline. We tell you — honestly — whether we're the right team and what it costs.
  2. 02 · Day 1–3
    Brief + treatment
    Written brief, shot list, and a reference board. One round of notes, then we lock.
  3. 03 · On site
    We show up, fully crewed
    Two-camera minimum, audio, lighting. We embed with your team — not background crew.
  4. 04 · +24h
    Ship
    Recap + 3–5 short-form cuts delivered within 24h of wrap. Color-graded, captioned, platform-ready.
